ABOUT

History

The guild Reclaimed was created by Bageri, Brasan, Bigcannon, Farmr and Happysmile back in Vanilla. Together they are joining for the rebirth of Reclaimed. Badgeri has lead Reclaimed throughout many years. During "The Burning Crusade" and "Wrath of the Lich King" the guild consistently placed amongst the top 250 guilds in the world, even though we had a very "casual" approach. We tried to keep the raids "quality over quantity". The final content we raided as a guild was "Warlords of Draenor", and since then the guild has been on a break. We're very happy to bring the guild together again for what is arguably still the best game ever made, World of Warcraft Classic.

Future

Most of us don't have the time we once had (or wish we had). Hence, our goal is for us to raid twice per week;
  • Wednesdays between 20:00-23:00
  • Sundays between 19:00-23:00
At release Molten Core and Onyxia will be available, and the first "major patch" will release Blackwing Lair and Zul'Gurub. A large part of the guild have raided at private vanilla servers, and I have no doubt that both MC and BWL will be quick to clear. ZG will be done as per-demand basis on offdays for those interested. And we aim to be clearing all available 40-man content in one evening within a short time.
